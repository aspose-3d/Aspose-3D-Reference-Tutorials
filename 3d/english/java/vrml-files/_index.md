## What is “convert VRML to OBJ”?
Converting VRML to OBJ means reading a VRML scene, interpreting its geometry, and writing that data into an OBJ file, which is compatible with most 3D engines, game frameworks, and rendering pipelines.

## Why Convert VRML to OBJ in Java?
